# 🌑 Moondark

> Cloud-based modding for mobile & console. No limits.

Moondark is the successor to Starlight. Instead of running mods directly on your device, everything runs on a remote PC environment and gets streamed straight to you. The result: no device bottlenecks, no compatibility nightmares, and mod complexity that wasn't possible before.

---

## How it works

1. You subscribe and connect your account
2. Mods run on our remote PC infrastructure
3. Gameplay is streamed to your mobile or console device in real time
4. You play — nothing heavy runs locally

---

## Platform support

- 📱 Mobile (iOS & Android)
- 🎮 Console

---

## Pricing

**$99/month** - one subscription, all supported platforms.

---

## Why we built this

Starlight was about making modding accessible. The problem was always the device itself - limited hardware, OS restrictions, security sandboxes. We were constantly fighting the platform just to get mods to run.

Moondark moves the fight off your device entirely. The remote environment has no OS restrictions, no hardware ceiling, and no gatekeeping. We control the stack, so we can actually build without limits.
